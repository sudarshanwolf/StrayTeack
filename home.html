Frequently Asked Questions

How does the tracking system At StrayTrack?

Our system uses a combination of QR codes and RFID tags that can be easily attached to a dog's collar. These tags contain unique identifiers that can be scanned to access the dog's complete profile and history.

Is the tracking device safe for dogs?

Yes, our tracking devices are completely safe and lightweight. They're designed specifically for dogs and have been tested extensively to ensure they cause no discomfort.

How can organizations partner with StrayTrack?

Organizations can reach out through our contact form or email us directly. We offer different partnership programs tailored to various needs and scales of operation.
